Why Shellharbour

A Great Place to Build a Business

It's not just the lifestyle — though that helps. Shellharbour has the population, the growth, and the community spirit to make businesses genuinely thrive.

A City in Growth

Shell Cove's masterplanned Waterfront precinct is transforming the region — new homes, hospitality venues, the Crown Plaza Hotel, and a marina drawing visitors from across NSW. This is a city with serious momentum, and new residents mean new customers.

Lifestyle That Attracts Talent

Coastal living, affordable housing relative to Sydney, and a relaxed community culture are drawing professionals and families to the region. For employers, that means a growing local talent pool. For consumer businesses, it means a rising, engaged customer base right on your doorstep.

A Community That Backs Its Own

Shellharbour businesses are known for supporting each other. The council's Economic Development Unit actively partners with state government, and groups like SHBNG exist precisely because local business owners wanted a space to connect, collaborate, and grow together.

Connected, Not Isolated

90 minutes to Sydney. Shellharbour Airport offering regional connections. Close to Wollongong's university, hospital, and services. You get the benefits of a close-knit regional community without being cut off from the broader economy.

Real Government Support

Shellharbour City Council has a dedicated Economic Development Unit. Service NSW Business Bureau offers free, personalised business advice. And with state and federal programs available for local businesses, there's genuine support at every stage — startup through to scale.

Quality of Life, Built In

Beaches, bushland, great coffee, and a coastal pace of life. Happy business owners make better decisions — and Shellharbour's lifestyle is genuinely one of its best business assets. It's easier to turn up and do great work when you love where you live.

Shell Cove & The Waterfront

Australia's Newest Marina Precinct

The Waterfront at Shell Cove is the centrepiece of Shellharbour's growth story — a masterplanned coastal precinct built around a brand-new marina. It's drawing visitors, new residents, and businesses to the region in a way that's genuinely reshaping the local economy.

For the businesses already here, it's new customers and new energy. For businesses considering the region, it's a signal: Shellharbour is investing in its future.

Support & Resources

You Don't Have to Figure It Out Alone

Between the council, state government, and the SHBNG community, there's more support available to Shellharbour businesses than most people realise. Here's where to start.

Shellharbour City Council — Economic Development

The council's Economic Development Unit supports local businesses with advice, connections, and partnerships. They run free one-hour business consultations in partnership with Service NSW and Sarina Russo Entrepreneurs — covering everything from funding access to business planning and compliance.

Visit Council Business Page

Service NSW Business Bureau

Free, personalised business support from the NSW Government. The Business Bureau helps with licences and permits, navigating government programs, finding grants and funding, NSW Government procurement opportunities, and specialist support for women, multicultural, and First Nations business owners.

Book a Free Consultation

Shellharbour & Beyond Networking Group

The fastest way to tap into the local business community. Our monthly networking mornings bring together business owners, professionals, and community leaders from across the Shellharbour region. First visit is always free — come and see what it's about.
