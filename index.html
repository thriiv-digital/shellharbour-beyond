About Us

Built for Shellharbour, Built for You

The Shellharbour & Beyond Networking Group was born from a simple idea: that local businesses are stronger together. After years of waiting for a dedicated networking space within Shellharbour City Council, we made it happen.

Our meetings are relaxed and welcoming — centred on real conversation, genuine connections, and community growth. Whether you're established or just starting out, there's a seat at the table for you.

Real Connections

Face-to-face relationships that go beyond a business card exchange.

Community First

Supporting local, lifting each other up, and building something meaningful together.

Inclusive by Design

Free to attend your first time. Open to all — businesses, charities, community leaders and more.

Who It's For

Everyone is Welcome

From established businesses to passionate newcomers — if you're invested in Shellharbour, this group is for you.

Business Owners

Sole traders, SMEs and local operators looking to grow their network and find collaborators.

Professionals

Consultants, creatives and specialists who want meaningful local connections.

Community Leaders

Councils, associations and leaders shaping the direction of the Shellharbour region.

Charities & NFPs

Not-for-profits looking to raise awareness and partner with local businesses.

New to the Area

Anyone who has recently moved to Shellharbour and wants to plug into the local community.

Just Curious

Not sure yet? Your first visit is completely free. Come along and see if it's the right fit.
