Membership Agreement

What we ask of members

By joining the Shellharbour & Beyond directory you agree to the following:

  • Your business is legitimately operating in or serving the Shellharbour region
  • The information in your listing is accurate and up to date
  • You will notify us of any significant changes to your business details
  • You conduct your business with integrity and respect toward other members
  • Annual renewal maintains your active listing — non-renewal will result in removal after a 14-day grace period

We reserve the right to decline or remove any listing that does not meet our community standards. Payment is non-refundable once a listing has been approved.
